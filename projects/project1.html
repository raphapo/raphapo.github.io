Sustainable House

A sleek, sustainable residential design

Project Overview

The Sustainable House project explores contemporary residential architecture with a focus on sustainability, open living spaces, and integration with the natural environment. The design uses eco-friendly materials and maximizes natural light, creating a comfortable and energy-efficient home.

Key Features

  • Open-plan living and dining areas
  • Large windows for natural lighting
  • Green roof and solar panels
  • Minimalist interior with natural materials
  • Seamless indoor-outdoor connection
