Responsive landscape proposal for the Norwich competition

Project Introduction

For the Norwich competition I developed an adaptable landscape scheme that responds to the site's history and ecology. The design integrates green corridors, community spaces and sustainable water management to create a vibrant public realm that could evolve over time.

Key Features

  • Linear green corridors linking existing parks
  • Flood-resilient planting and bioswales
  • Community lawns and event spaces
  • Wildlife-friendly native species
  • Flexible layout that can adapt to future phases
